## January 2025

### Meta Ads Integration

- **Launch campaigns directly** to Facebook and Instagram
- OAuth-based connection to Meta Business accounts
- Select ad account and page for each campaign
- Automatic placement optimization

### AI Image Editing

- **Natural language editing** - describe changes and AI applies them
- Create variations from existing images
- Iterative refinement with edit history

### Multi-Provider Generation

- Generate images from multiple AI providers simultaneously
- Automatic selection of best results
- Support for DALL-E 3, Imagen 3, and Gemini

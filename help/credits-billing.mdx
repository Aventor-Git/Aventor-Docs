## How Credits Work

Aventor uses a credit-based system for AI image generation.

### Credit Usage

| Action | Credits Used |
|--------|--------------|
| Generate images (4 variations) | 1 credit |
| Edit an image | 1 credit |
| Brand enrichment (URL analysis) | Free |
| Download images | Free |
| Campaign launch | Free |

<Note>
Credit costs may vary based on resolution and AI provider. Higher resolutions may use additional credits.
</Note>

## Billing FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do credits expire?">
    Credit expiration policies will be communicated when pricing launches. Typically, credits remain valid for the duration of your subscription.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Refund policies will be published with pricing details. Generally, unused credits may be refundable within a certain period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I run out of credits?">
    You'll be prompted to purchase more credits before generating additional images. Your existing generations and profiles remain accessible.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do campaign launches cost credits?">
    No. Launching campaigns to Meta or Google is free. You only pay for image generation and editing. Ad spend goes directly to the ad platforms.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Demo mode allows you to explore the platform with limited generations. Full trial details coming with pricing launch.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Attachment Limits

### File Size Limits

| Limit | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Max file size | 10 MB per file |
| Max attachments | 10 files per session |
| Max total size | 50 MB per session |

### Supported Formats

**Images:**
- PNG
- JPG / JPEG
- WebP
- GIF (static frame used)

<Warning>
Files exceeding size limits will be rejected. Compress large images before uploading.
</Warning>

## Generation Limits

### Per Session

| Limit | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Max generations | Based on your plan |
| Max variations | 4 per generation |
| Max edits | 10 per image |

### Resolution Options

| Format | Dimensions | Use Case |
|--------|------------|----------|
| Square | 2K base (2048px) | Instagram feed |
| Portrait | 2K base (varies by aspect ratio) | Facebook/Instagram feed |
| Landscape | 2K base (varies by aspect ratio) | Website banners |
| Story | 2K base (varies by aspect ratio) | Stories and Reels |

## Rate Limits

To ensure fair usage and system stability:

- **Generations**: Limited requests per minute
- **Enrichment**: Limited URL analyses per minute
- **API calls**: Standard rate limiting applies

<Note>
If you hit a rate limit, wait a moment and try again. Limits reset automatically.
</Note>

## Content Guidelines

### Allowed Content

Aventor generates ads for legitimate businesses including:
- Products and services
- E-commerce and retail
- Professional services
- Food and beverage
- Technology and software
- Health and wellness (within guidelines)

### Prohibited Content

The following content types are not allowed:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Illegal products or services">
    - Illegal drugs or drug paraphernalia
    - Weapons and ammunition
    - Counterfeit goods
    - Stolen property
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Adult content">
    - Explicit sexual content
    - Adult entertainment services
    - Nudity beyond artistic context
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Harmful content">
    - Hate speech or discrimination
    - Violence or graphic content
    - Harassment or bullying
    - Self-harm promotion
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Deceptive practices">
    - Misleading health claims
    - Get-rich-quick schemes
    - Fake testimonials
    - Phishing or scam content
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Restricted categories">
    - Tobacco and vaping products
    - Gambling (varies by jurisdiction)
    - Political advertising
    - Cryptocurrency (restricted)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
Violating content guidelines may result in account suspension. Generated content must also comply with Meta and Google ad policies for campaign launch.
</Warning>

## AI Generation Safety

### Built-in Safeguards

Aventor's AI providers include safety measures:
- Content filters for prohibited material
- Automatic rejection of harmful prompts
- Human likeness protections (for some providers)

### Text in Images

<Info>
AI-generated text in images may contain errors. Always review text carefully before launching campaigns.
</Info>

Best practices:
- Keep text minimal
- Use your uploaded logo instead of generated text
- Edit or add text overlays post-export for accuracy

## Platform Policies

When launching campaigns, your ads must comply with platform policies:

### Meta Ads Policies

Meta reviews all ads for:
- Policy compliance
- Image quality
- Text-to-image ratio (some placements)
- Landing page quality

[View Meta Advertising Standards](https://www.facebook.com/policies/ads/)

### Google Ads Policies

Google reviews ads for:
- Prohibited content
- Restricted content
- Editorial standards
- Technical requirements

[View Google Ads Policies](https://support.google.com/adspolicy)

## Data & Privacy

### What We Store

- Your brand profiles and descriptions
- Generated images (encrypted storage)
- Campaign configurations
- Usage analytics

### What We Don't Store

- Your ad platform passwords
- Payment card details (handled by payment processor)
- Personal data beyond account info

### Data Retention

- Active account data: Retained while account is active
- Deleted content: Removed within 30 days
- Account deletion: All data removed within 30 days

<Tip>
You can download your generated images at any time. Export before deleting if you want to keep copies.
</Tip>

## Campaign Objectives

Your campaign objective determines how Meta/Google optimizes ad delivery.

## Goal Types

### Awareness

<Card title="Best for" icon="eye">
New brands, product launches, reaching new audiences
</Card>

**What it does:**
- Optimizes for reach and impressions
- Shows ads to people most likely to remember them
- Maximizes brand visibility

**When to use:**
- Launching a new product or brand
- Building recognition in a new market
- Reaching the widest possible audience

### Consideration

<Card title="Best for" icon="hand-pointer">
Driving traffic, engagement, video views
</Card>

**What it does:**
- Optimizes for link clicks and engagement
- Shows ads to people likely to take action
- Drives website visits and interactions

**When to use:**
- Driving traffic to your website
- Promoting content or blog posts
- Getting people to engage with your brand

### Conversion

<Card title="Best for" icon="shopping-cart">
Sales, leads, app installs
</Card>

**What it does:**
- Optimizes for specific conversion events
- Shows ads to people most likely to convert
- Focuses on bottom-of-funnel actions

**When to use:**
- Driving sales or sign-ups
- Lead generation campaigns
- Retargeting warm audiences

## Goal Selection Tips

<Tip>
**New brands**: Start with Awareness to build recognition, then retarget with Conversion campaigns.
</Tip>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Match goal to funnel stage">
    - **Top of funnel** → Awareness
    - **Middle of funnel** → Consideration
    - **Bottom of funnel** → Conversion
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Consider your audience size">
    - Small, warm audiences → Conversion
    - Large, cold audiences → Awareness first
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Align with your KPIs">
    - Measuring reach? → Awareness
    - Measuring clicks? → Consideration
    - Measuring sales? → Conversion
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How Aventor Uses Goals

Your selected goal influences:
- **Generated prompts** - AI creates visuals suited to your objective
- **Campaign setup** - Default settings optimized for your goal
- **Audience suggestions** - Targeting recommendations based on objective

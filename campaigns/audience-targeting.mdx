## Targeting Options

Define your ideal audience using demographics, interests, and behaviors.

## Demographics

### Age Range
Select the age range for your target audience:
- 18-24
- 25-34
- 35-44
- 45-54
- 55-64
- 65+

### Gender
- All genders
- Men
- Women

### Location
Target by:
- Countries
- Regions/States
- Cities
- Postal codes
- Radius around a location

## Interests

Target users based on:
- Pages they've liked
- Content they engage with
- Behaviors and activities
- Purchase intent signals

<Tip>
Aventor suggests interests based on your brand analysis. Review and adjust based on your actual customer profile.
</Tip>

## Custom Audiences

<Note>
Custom audiences require additional Meta Business setup. These options are available in Meta Ads Manager after your campaign launches.
</Note>

Available custom audience types:
- **Customer lists** - Upload email lists
- **Website visitors** - Retarget site visitors (requires Meta Pixel)
- **Lookalike audiences** - Find similar users to your customers

## Aventor Recommendations

Based on your brand analysis, Aventor suggests:
- **Target audience** - Inferred from your website content
- **Age range** - Based on your product type
- **Interest categories** - Derived from your industry

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How are recommendations generated?">
    Aventor analyzes your website content, product type, and brand messaging to infer your likely target audience. These are starting points - adjust based on your actual customer data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I override recommendations?">
    Yes. All targeting fields are editable. Use Aventor's suggestions as a starting point and customize for your needs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Start broad, then narrow">
    Meta's algorithm often performs better with broader targeting. Let the platform find your best customers, then create lookalikes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Avoid over-targeting">
    Narrow audiences can limit reach and increase costs. Balance specificity with scale.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Test different audiences">
    Create multiple ad sets with different targeting to find what works best.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

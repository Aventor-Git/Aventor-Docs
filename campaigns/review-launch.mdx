## Pre-Launch Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Creative">
    Confirm selected images are correct and on-brand.

    Check:
    - Logo placement is appropriate
    - Product is clearly visible
    - No unwanted artifacts or errors
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Targeting">
    Check audience settings match your target customers.

    Review:
    - Age range
    - Location
    - Interests
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Budget">
    Review daily/lifetime spend and duration.

    Verify:
    - Budget amount is correct
    - Duration matches your goals
    - Platform allocation is set
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Account">
    Ensure Meta or Google Ads account is connected.

    Required:
    - Valid payment method
    - Ad account in good standing
    - Proper permissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Click **Launch** to submit your campaign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Launch Confirmation

After clicking Launch:

1. **Campaign submitted** - Your campaign is sent to the ad platform
2. **Review period** - Platform reviews your ads (typically 24 hours for Meta)
3. **Approval** - Once approved, ads start delivering
4. **Monitoring** - Track performance in your ad platform dashboard

<Note>
Aventor creates the campaign but does not manage ongoing optimization. Use Meta Ads Manager or Google Ads for performance monitoring and adjustments.
</Note>

## Review Requirements

### Meta Ads Review

Meta reviews all ads for:
- Policy compliance
- Image quality
- Text-to-image ratio (for some placements)
- Landing page quality

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Common rejection reasons">
    - Misleading claims
    - Prohibited content
    - Poor landing page experience
    - Excessive text in images
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review timeline">
    Most ads are reviewed within 24 hours. Complex cases may take longer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## After Launch

### Monitoring

Track your campaign in:
- **Meta Ads Manager** - For Facebook/Instagram campaigns
- **Google Ads Dashboard** - For Google campaigns (coming soon)

### Key Metrics

- **Impressions** - How many times your ad was shown
- **Reach** - Unique people who saw your ad
- **Clicks** - Actions taken on your ad
- **CTR** - Click-through rate
- **Conversions** - Completed goals

### Optimization

<Tip>
Let campaigns run for at least 3-7 days before making major changes. Algorithms need time to optimize delivery.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Campaign stuck in review">
    Meta reviews typically complete within 24 hours. If longer, check for policy warnings in Ads Manager.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ads not delivering">
    Common causes: low budget, narrow audience, billing issues. Check your Ads Manager for specific warnings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Poor performance">
    Try different creatives, adjust targeting, or increase budget. A/B test to find what works.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

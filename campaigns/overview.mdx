## Campaign Flow

After creating your ads in the Studio, launch them directly to Meta or Google.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Ads">
    Choose which generated images to include in your campaign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Goals">
    Define your campaign objective (Awareness, Traffic, Conversions).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Audience">
    Set targeting parameters for your ideal customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allocate Budget">
    Set daily or lifetime budget and duration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Launch">
    Review all settings and launch your campaign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | Status | Features |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram) | Live | Full campaign creation and launch |
| Google Ads (Search/PMax) | Coming Soon | Placeholder |

## Campaign Types

### Meta Ads

Launch to Facebook and Instagram with:
- Automatic creative optimization
- Interest and demographic targeting
- Daily or lifetime budgets
- Multiple placements (Feed, Stories, Reels)

### Google Ads

<Info>
Google Ads integration is coming soon. Currently you can download images and upload them manually to Google Ads.
</Info>

## Before You Start

Ensure you have:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="For Meta Ads" icon="meta">
    - Meta Business account
    - Active Ad Account
    - Facebook Page connected to your Business
    - Payment method on file
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="For Google Ads" icon="google">
    - Google Ads account (coming soon)
    - Billing set up
    - Campaign budget defined
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

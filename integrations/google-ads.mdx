## Planned Features

### Search Campaigns

- Text ads with AI-generated headlines and descriptions
- Keyword targeting based on your brand and audience
- Automated bidding strategies

### Performance Max Campaigns

- Multi-format campaigns across Google properties
- Use generated images as creative assets
- Automated placements (Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail, Maps)

### Display Campaigns

- Banner ads with generated creatives
- Audience targeting and remarketing
- Responsive display ad formats

## Current Workaround

Until Google Ads integration is live, you can:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate Images">
    Create ad visuals in the Aventor Studio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    Export images using the download button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload to Google Ads">
    Upload images directly in [Google Ads](https://ads.google.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Campaign">
    Set up targeting, bidding, and budget in Google Ads interface.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You'll Need

When Google Ads integration launches, you'll need:

- Google Ads account
- Billing configured in Google Ads
- At least one active campaign type available

## Prerequisites

- A website URL or brand description
- (Optional) Brand assets: logo, product images, style references

## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Aventor">
    Go to [aventor.ai](https://aventor.ai) and sign in or try the demo.

    <Tip>
    Demo mode gives you 3 free generations to try before signing up.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Brand">
    Paste your website URL or describe your brand in the text box.

    Aventor analyzes your site to extract:
    - Brand colors and palette
    - Key messaging and value props
    - Visual style and tone
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Campaign Goals">
    Choose your objective:
    - **Awareness** - Reach new audiences
    - **Consideration** - Drive traffic and engagement
    - **Conversion** - Generate sales and leads
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Ads">
    Click **Generate** to create multiple ad variations.

    Each generation uses multiple AI providers for maximum variety.

    <Note>
    Generation may take up to 2 minutes as we call multiple AI providers in parallel.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit & Export">
    Select your favorites, make edits with natural language, and download or launch directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What is Brand Enrichment?

When you enter a website URL, Aventor's AI analyzes your site to extract key brand elements:

- **Color Palette** - Primary and secondary brand colors
- **Messaging** - Key value propositions and taglines
- **Tone** - Brand voice and communication style
- **Visual Style** - Photography style, composition preferences

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="URL Analysis">
    Aventor fetches and parses your website content and images.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Processing">
    GPT-4 analyzes the content to understand your brand identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Context Building">
    Extracted elements are used to guide image generation prompts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Gets Extracted

| Element | Description | Used For |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Colors | Primary, secondary, accent colors | Image color palette |
| Messaging | Value props, taglines, benefits | Generation prompts |
| Tone | Formal, casual, playful, etc. | Visual mood |
| Audience | Target demographic | Style matching |

## Manual Description

If you don't have a website, you can describe your brand directly:

```
We're a sustainable coffee brand targeting eco-conscious millennials.
Our colors are forest green and cream. We emphasize organic,
fair-trade sourcing with a warm, inviting tone.
```

<Tip>
Include details about your target audience, key differentiators, and visual preferences for best results.
</Tip>

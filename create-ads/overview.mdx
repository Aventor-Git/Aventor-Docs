## The Aventor Studio

The Studio is your central workspace for creating, editing, and managing AI-generated ads.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/studio-overview.png" alt="Aventor Studio interface showing the main workspace" />
</Frame>

## Key Areas

### Chat Panel

Enter your website URL or brand description. Aventor uses this to understand your brand and generate relevant ads.

Features:
- URL input with automatic brand analysis
- Manual brand description support
- Asset attachment buttons
- Campaign goal selection

### Design Panel

View and select from generated ad variations. Each generation produces multiple options from different AI providers.

Features:
- Grid view of generated images
- Provider badges (OpenAI, Gemini, Vertex)
- Selection checkboxes for batch operations
- Quick preview on hover

### Edit Mode

Make changes to any image using natural language. Describe what you want to change and AI applies the edit.

Features:
- Natural language editing
- Before/after comparison
- Variation creation
- Edit history

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Brand Input">
    Enter your website URL or describe your brand in the chat panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enrichment">
    Aventor analyzes your brand to extract colors, messaging, and style.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generation">
    AI creates multiple ad variations based on your brand context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Selection">
    Choose your favorites from the generated options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Editing">
    Refine selected images with natural language edits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Download images or launch directly to ad platforms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

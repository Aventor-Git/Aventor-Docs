## What is Aventor?

Aventor is an AI-powered ad creation platform that generates Facebook, Instagram, and Google ads from your website URL or brand description. Enter your website, and Aventor handles the rest.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Your Website">
    Paste your URL or describe your brand. Aventor analyzes your brand automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Ads">
    AI creates multiple ad variations using your brand colors, logo, and messaging.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit & Refine">
    Use natural language to edit images. Add your assets for branded results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Campaigns">
    Connect Meta or Google Ads to launch directly from Aventor.
  </Step>
</Steps>
